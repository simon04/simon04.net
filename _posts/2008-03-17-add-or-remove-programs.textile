Using Windows I often need to get the _Add or Remove Programs_ screen. Navigating through _Start _» _Settings _» _Control Panel _» _Add or Remove Programs_ is unhandy. Just get the Run dialog by pressing <code>[Win] + [R]</code> and enter <code>appwiz.cpl</code>.
