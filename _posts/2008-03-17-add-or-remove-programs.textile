Using Windows I often need to get the <em>Add or Remove Programs</em> screen. Navigating through <em>Start </em>» <em>Settings </em>» <em>Control Panel </em>» <em>Add or Remove Programs</em> is unhandy. Just get the Run dialog by pressing <em>[Win] + [R]</em> and enter <em><strong>appwiz.cpl</strong></em>.
